How this table works

The play mode is chosen automatically when you press “Start”, depending on how many human players are seated.

Choose your setup

Group Play (2+ humans)

Each human enters a name. After the hand starts, every human seat shows a QR code for the private hole-card view and a direct link to the remote table view.

Actions can still happen on the shared table, or from the linked device when that seat is active.

Empty seats are automatically filled by bots, so mixed human/bot tables work without any setup.

Solo vs Bots (1 human)

One human plays directly on the main device. No QR scan is required and that player’s hole cards remain visible on the table.

Spectator / TV Poker (0 humans)

All seats are filled by bots. The table runs automatically and all hole cards remain visible.

Before you start

  • Enter player names in the name badges.
  • Leave a seat empty if you want it filled by a bot.
  • Use the close icon to remove seats you do not need.
  • Use the rotate icon to orient a seat toward the player sitting there. This is especially useful on tablets.
  • You need at least 2 active seats before the game can start.

During a hand

  • Dealer and blinds are assigned automatically, and the blind level increases over time.
  • The table uses standard poker action labels: Fold, Check, Call, Raise, All-in.
  • Slider guide: 0 = Check, matching the current bet = Call, above that = Raise, maximum = All-in.
  • Raises that are too small turn red while dragging and snap to the minimum legal raise when released.

Read the table

  • The pot is shown in the center. Community cards are in the middle, and each seat displays its stack and current round bet.
  • Dealer, small blind, and big blind icons indicate the current positions.
  • The notification bar is clickable and opens the hand log.
  • Stats and Log become available after completed hands.
  • Win percentages and short hand labels only appear when hole cards are visible on the shared table, such as in spectator mode or during all-in runouts.
